Monitoring Parameters

Monitoring parameters are critical to ensuring the smooth operation of any diesel generator. By keeping a close eye on factors such as fuel levels, pressure etc., a monitoring system can help prevent downtime and costly repairs.

Run hours of DG set

Running hours of DG set both month wise and day wise can be calculated and the same is displayed in the form of graph in the FuelTM app.

Calculate running time

Based on the generator’s fuel consumption rate, fuel tank capacity and current fuel level, FuelTM app can estimate the remaining run time of generators.

Fault and Alert detection

FuelTM monitors the fuel system and analyses data to detect anomalies or irregularities. When an issue is detected, app will issue an alert to the user.

Benefits

A generator fuel monitoring system provides real-time information on fuel usage, helping to optimize fuel efficiency and reduce costs. It also alerts operators to potential issues such as fuel theft, leakage or engine inefficiencies, enabling prompt action to prevent further damage.

Improved Fuel Efficiency

A generator fuel monitoring app can help you track your generator's fuel consumption and identify any inefficiencies or wastage. By identifying and addressing these issues, you can save money on fuel costs.

Enhanced Maintenance

By tracking your generator's fuel consumption, you can identify when it is time for maintenance or repairs. This can help you avoid costly breakdowns and ensure that your generator is always running at optimal levels.

Enhanced safety

A generator fuel monitoring app can help you identify any safety issues related to fuel usage, such as leaks or spills. This can help you address these issues promptly, minimizing the risk of accidents or environmental damage.

Data Analytics

The app can provide you with insights into fuel usage patterns and generator performance over time, enabling you to make informed decisions and adjustments to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
